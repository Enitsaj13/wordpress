MCGI Free Store<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

In all the charitable endeavors of the Members Church of God International (MCGI), the ultimate objective is to glorify the name of the Almighty God.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

\u201cLet your light so shine before men, that they may see your good works, and glorify your Father which is in heaven.\u201d (Matthew 5:16, KJV)<\/em><\/p><\/blockquote>\n\n\n\n

Among these endeavors is the MCGI Free Store where Jane Domin-Ang and thousands of others have shopped for free. Her statement is just one proof that the Church\u2019s objective of making God\u2019s name exalted is fulfilled.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

What is the MCGI Free Store?<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

The MCGI Free Store is exactly what its name implies \u2014 a store where everything is free. 
Shelves at MCGI Free Store branches are usually stacked with essential grocery items \u2014 rice, bread, canned goods, snacks, coffee and milk packets, condiments, instant noodles, toiletries, soap, and many more. 
Most branches also offer brand new or used clothes, footwear, bags, and toys; in other locations, fresh fruits, vegetables and raw meat are up for grabs.<\/p>\n\n\n
