Church Ministries<\/h2>\n\n\n\n

Overview<\/h4>\n\n\n\n

We at Members Church of God International have many ministries. We share one goal: spread God\u2019s words.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We are service-oriented. We help families and communities around the world. Our members work faithfully to serve you, our fellow men, and God above all.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Branches<\/h4>\n\n\n\n

Propagation<\/h5>\n\n\n\n

This Ministry aims to preach the Gospel. It serves those who desire to learn God\u2019s words.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Our Elders are sent around the world to seed the Gospel. They help the Church in all kinds of propagation services.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Broadcast<\/h5>\n\n\n\n

The Broadcast Ministry works to bring the word of God to you through technology. It produces propagation materials in many forms.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

This Ministry is the Church\u2019s hand in bringing the Gospel to the world.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Foreign<\/h5>\n\n\n\n

The Foreign Ministry is the Church\u2019s ambassador. It looks after the needs of the Church\u2019s global community.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Our people here work hard to build and keep locales around the world. They also give charity services. They provide socio-civic groups with volunteers.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Prison<\/h5>\n\n\n\n

The Prison Ministry aims to reach people behind bars. Our friends in the Ministry set Bible studies and give aid to prisons.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

This Ministry helps inmates achieve spiritual peace and freedom.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Youth<\/h5>\n\n\n\n

The Youth Ministry is composed of members at their prime. The youth comprise the largest group in the Church.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Our young members are grouped based on skills and interests. This Ministry is a big source of workforce for the Church.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Flock of Lambs (Kawan ng Cordero)<\/h5>\n\n\n\n

The children in the Church compose the Flock of Lambs.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The Flock of Lambs are taught God\u2019s teachings at an early age. This is based on the teaching in the Book of Proverbs.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Children learn Bible stories and Christian values through weekly classes. This group has an award-winning program on Philippine TV.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Music Ministry<\/h5>\n\n\n\n

The Music Ministry leads in the singing of hymns during gatherings. The Christian Theater Group uses their stage skills to glorify God.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We believe that music brings people closer to the Lord.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Servants\u2019 Ministry<\/h5>\n\n\n\n

The Servants\u2019 Ministry help preach God\u2019s words. They also give spiritual advice to both members and non-members.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Our Church servants are sent worldwide. They learn the country\u2019s language to share Christ\u2019s doctrines.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
